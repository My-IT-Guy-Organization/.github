# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all, regardless of experience level.

## Our Standards

*   Use welcoming and inclusive language.

*   Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.

*   Focus on what is best for the community and our clients.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at [garrettadams1010+conduct@gmail.com](mailto:garrettadams1010+conduct@gmail.com).
